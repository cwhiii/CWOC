# Notes View

Multi-column masonry layout with full rendered markdown. `[[chit title]]` auto-links to other chits.

**Click** on a note's text area to edit it in place. **Shift+click** (or long-press ~1.2s on mobile) opens the Quick Edit modal. A short hold (~0.4s) then drag lets you reorder notes between columns. While editing, drag-and-drop reordering is disabled so you can click to position the cursor. Press `ESC` or click outside to save and exit edit mode.
