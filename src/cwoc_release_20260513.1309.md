Added the Omni View — a command-center dashboard fusing today's itinerary, pinned chits, and unread email into a single actionable view. Activated by clicking "Omni" in the header or pressing O. Features an HST timeline bar with weather icons, configurable 8-section two-column layout (Chrono Anchored, On Deck, Soon, Email, Pinned Notes, Pinned Checklists, Weather Bar, HST Bar), email pagination from Omni-enabled bundles, filter locking, habit streak counters, time-until badges, and full Settings page layout configurator with drag-to-reorder. Hotkeys reshuffled: O → Omni View, S → Sort, F9 → Settings.
